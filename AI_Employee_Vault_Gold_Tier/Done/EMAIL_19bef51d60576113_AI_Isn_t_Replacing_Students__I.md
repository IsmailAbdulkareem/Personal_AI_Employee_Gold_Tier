Everyone is worried about AI replacing students. That’s the wrong fear. The real problem? Most learning systems were already broken before AI arrived. Students were memorizing instead of understanding. Chasing grades instead of clarity. Burning hours instead of building skills. AI didn’t create this problem — it exposed it. Used wrongly, AI becomes a shortcut. Used correctly, AI becomes a thinking partner. At UAARN, we’re not building tools that give answers. We’re building systems that help students: Think critically Stay consistent Learn with intent This newsletter isn’t about hype. It’s about rethinking education for the AI era — honestly, practically, and human-first. In the next editions, I’ll share: What students actually need from AI Lessons from building UAARN How to learn smarter in a noisy world If this resonates, you’re in the right place. — Maryam Arif, Founder & CEO, Nexe-Agent
